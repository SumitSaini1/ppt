The Problem with Parkinson's

  • What is Parkinson's (PD)? It's a progressive brain disorder that affects movement and speech.
  • The Challenge: Traditional diagnosis is often late, happening only after major symptoms appear.
  • Why Early Detection Matters: Catching PD early is key to managing the disease and improving a patient's quality of life.

Our Solution: MediScan AI

What is it?

An AI-powered healthcare portal.

How does it help?

It spots early signs of Parkinson's using simple, non-invasive tests.

What data does it use?

Voice recordings and handwriting samples.

The Goal

To make early screening accurate, easy, and accessible to everyone.

Key Objectives

  • Build smart AI models to analyze speech and handwriting for PD risk.
  • Create a secure and easy-to-use web portal for patients and doctors.
  • Allow users to upload and manage their medical records.
  • Include tools for booking appointments and secure doctor-patient communication.
  • Provide clear, instant reports on screening results to help doctors make decisions.

The Technology Stack

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript

Backend

Python (Flask)

Database

MySQL

Machine Learning

Scikit-learn (Random Forest, SVM)

Audio & Image

Librosa (Audio), OpenCV (Image)

Core Features (Part 1)

  • 1. Speech Analysis:
    • Users record their voice.
    • The system analyzes it for tiny changes in pitch, jitter (vocal tremor), and shimmer.
    • An AI model then gives a risk score for PD.
  • 2. Handwriting Analysis:
    • Users upload images of their writing (like a signature or a spiral).
    • The system uses OpenCV to look for tremors or irregularities.
    • This helps assess motor impairment.

Core Features (Part 2)

  • 3. Health Records Upload:
    Securely upload medical documents (PDFs, images). Creates an organized health timeline for the patient.
  • 4. Doctor Collaboration:
    Doctors can securely log in to see patient reports, add clinical notes, e-prescriptions, and send encrypted messages.
  • 5. Appointment Management:
    A simple system to book, reschedule, or cancel appointments. Includes reminders.

How It Works (The User's Journey)

  1. 1. Register & Login: Patient or doctor creates a secure account.
  2. 2. Upload Samples: Patient uploads a voice recording and a handwriting sample.
  3. 3. AI Analysis: Our Flask backend processes the files. Librosa & OpenCV extract key features. The AI models predict the risk of PD.
  4. 4. View Results: The patient gets a clear, easy-to-understand report on their dashboard.
  5. 5. Consult: The doctor reviews the report, adds notes, and consults with the patient on the platform.

Dataset & Model Training

  • Data Source: We used publicly available datasets from Kaggle.
  • Data Contents: Voice and handwriting samples from both healthy controls and diagnosed PD patients.
  • Result: The models were trained to be highly accurate in telling the difference between healthy and PD-affected samples.

Conclusion & Future Work

  • What We've Built: A complete system that uses AI on voice and handwriting to help screen for Parkinson's early.
  • The Impact: It makes screening easier, faster, and more accessible. It also helps doctors and patients work together better.
  • What's Next? Combine speech, handwriting, and clinical data for even better accuracy. Build a mobile app (iOS/Android). Add telemedicine features.
